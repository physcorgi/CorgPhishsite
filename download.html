Frequently Asked Questions

Is CorgPhish free to use?

Yes, CorgPhish is completely free and open-source. You can use it for personal or commercial purposes without any limitations. The core functionality will always remain free, though we may introduce premium features in the future.

How does CorgPhish detect phishing attempts?

CorgPhish uses a combination of machine learning algorithms, URL analysis, content inspection, and visual similarity detection to identify potential phishing attempts. Our models are continuously updated based on the latest phishing techniques and threats.

Does CorgPhish collect my personal data?

CorgPhish respects your privacy. By default, it only collects anonymous usage statistics to improve the service. You can opt out of this data collection in the settings. We never collect personal information such as browsing history, passwords, or other sensitive data.

Can I use CorgPhish in my company?

Absolutely! CorgPhish can be deployed across your organization to protect all employees. For enterprise-level deployments with centralized management, please contact us for more information about our enterprise solutions.

How can I contribute to the project?

As an open-source project, we welcome contributions! You can contribute by submitting bug reports, feature requests, or code contributions through our GitHub repository. Check out our contribution guidelines for more information.
